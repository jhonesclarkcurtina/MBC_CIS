ABOUT OUR CHURCH

Church Information System

Church Information System (CIS)

Version 1.0.0

Digitally organizing church members to strengthen care groups and support church ministry.

Church Mission

To serve God faithfully by nurturing believers, strengthening fellowship, and building strong care groups through organized and responsible stewardship of church information.

Church System Purpose

This system is designed to help the church:

  • Organize member records responsibly
  • Manage care groups effectively
  • Support leaders and secretaries in their ministry
  • Maintain accurate and secure church information

System Features

  • Member Management & Organization
  • Care Group Management
  • Role-Based Access Control
  • Ministry Tracking
  • Secure Authentication
  • Light & Dark Mode
  • Responsive Design
  • Settings Management

User Roles

Admin

Full access to all system features, including user management and church settings.

Care Group Leader

View and manage members within their assigned care group.

Viewer / Secretary

View all members and add or update member information.

Technology Used

  • Backend: Python Flask
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Database: SQLite
  • Security: Password Hashing & Session Management

Development Note

This system was built to support church ministry, promote accountability, and assist church leaders in serving God's people more effectively.
